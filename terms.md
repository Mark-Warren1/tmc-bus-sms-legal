# Terms and Conditions — TMC Bus Ministry SMS Program

**Effective date:** April 22, 2026

## Program name

TMC Bus Ministry Scheduling

## Program description

This SMS program sends scheduling messages to volunteers who have agreed to participate in the weekly Wednesday bus ministry of The Mailbox Club International. Messages include scheduling requests, confirmations, and reminders.

## Message frequency

You may receive approximately 1-3 messages per week while participating in the rotation.

## Message and data rates

**Message and data rates may apply.** Check with your mobile carrier for details about your plan.

## Opt-out instructions

**Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe.** You may also request removal using natural language (e.g., "take me off the list"). Once opted out, you will not receive further messages.

## Help instructions

**Reply HELP for assistance**, or contact the program operator directly at mark16810@gmail.com.

## Supported carriers

This program is supported on all major US carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and their subsidiaries. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

## Eligibility

This program is for volunteers who have verbally agreed to participate in the bus ministry rotation. You must be 18 or older to participate.
