# Privacy Policy — TMC Bus Ministry SMS Program

**Effective date:** April 22, 2026
**Program operator:** Mark W., on behalf of The Mailbox Club International (Valdosta, GA)
**Contact:** mark16810@gmail.com

## What data we collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the bus ministry scheduling system:

- Your name
- Your mobile phone number
- Your role (driver or rider) and insurance status
- A record of past scheduling interactions (dates asked, dates served, responses)

We do not collect financial information, location data, browsing data, or any other personal information through this SMS program.

## How we use your data

Your information is used solely to:

- Send you scheduling requests, confirmations, and reminders for the weekly bus ministry
- Track fair rotation so the same volunteers are not asked too frequently
- Allow you to look up your assigned partner for any given week

## Sharing with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing purposes. The only third-party services that process your data are the infrastructure providers required to deliver the service:

- Twilio (SMS carrier)
- Google (Google Sheets, Gmail)
- Anthropic (AI-based message parsing)

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and only process your data on our behalf to deliver the scheduling service.

## Message frequency

You can expect approximately 1-3 messages per week during active scheduling.

## Opting out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message, or by asking to be removed in natural language (e.g., "take me off the list"). Upon opt-out, no further messages will be sent.

## Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as you are an active volunteer. Upon opt-out, your name and phone number are marked inactive but retained in case you wish to rejoin. You may request complete deletion by emailing mark16810@gmail.com.

## Questions

For any questions about this policy, contact mark16810@gmail.com.
